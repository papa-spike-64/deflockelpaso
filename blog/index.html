April 28, 2026 Contract Renewal Latest

The Flock contract was renewed. Here is what happened and what comes next.

On April 28, 2026, El Paso administratively renewed its Flock Safety contract through an MVCPA grant — $693,334 in grant funds plus $138,666 in El Paso tax dollars. An amendment requires open vendor selection for the next grant. Nothing about the underlying problems changed.
